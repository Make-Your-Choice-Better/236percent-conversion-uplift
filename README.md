# 📱 Proptech Lead Funnel Optimization — Channel Shift Case Study

> **Increased CTA conversion from 22% → 74%** by identifying a channel-audience mismatch and redirecting spend to WhatsApp.

---

## 🧩 The Problem

A PropTech client was running lead generation campaigns with a dedicated email CTA touchpoint on one of their lead sources. Despite consistent traffic, the **CTA conversion rate was stuck at 22%** — well below industry benchmarks for warm leads.

---

## 🔍 The Insight

A deep dive into the lead registration data revealed a critical mismatch:

| Metric | Finding |
|--------|---------|
| Primary registration method | 📞 Phone Number |
| Marketing channel in use | 📧 Email |
| Email engagement rate | Very Low |
| Audience digital behavior | Mobile-first, chat-native |

> **Key Insight:** The audience was registering via phone — signaling they were mobile-first users. Yet the CTA follow-up was happening over email, a channel they barely engaged with. The medium and the audience were misaligned.

---

## ⚡ The Fix

Shifted the CTA touchpoint from **Email → WhatsApp** for this lead source segment.

**Why WhatsApp worked:**
- Same audience already on mobile
- Higher open rates vs email (98% vs ~20%)
- Conversational format matched buying intent stage
- Frictionless CTA tapping on mobile

---

## 📈 Results

| Metric | Before | After | Change |
|--------|--------|-------|--------|
| CTA Conversion Rate | 22% | 74% | **+236% lift** |
| Channel | Email | WhatsApp | — |
| Marketing Spend Efficiency | Baseline | 3.4x improvement | — |

---

## 🛠 Methodology

1. **Funnel Audit** — Mapped every touchpoint from lead source to CTA
2. **Data Analysis** — Cross-referenced registration method with channel performance
3. **Hypothesis Formation** — Phone-registered users = mobile-first = low email intent
4. **Channel Experiment** — Switched CTA medium to WhatsApp for this segment
5. **Measurement** — Tracked conversion rate over campaign window
6. **Scale** — Validated and recommended full channel shift for this segment

---

## 💡 Transferable Framework

This case demonstrates a simple but powerful diagnostic:

```
If [registration behavior] ≠ [follow-up channel behavior]
→ You have a channel-audience mismatch
→ Fix the channel, not the message
```

Use this audit for any lead source where CTA performance is underperforming.
